Circular Artes-Visuales

Cera Circular es un conjunto de diversas ramas del arte. Un abanico de disciplinas que van desde lo gráfico, lo plástico, el dibujo y lo cerámico. Estos son algunos de los trabajos realizados a lo largo de varios años, algunos hechos a pedido, otros formaron parte de mi educación artística y otros hechos por puro placer.

MINERVA

The Gods have abandoned me, now there is no hope. And she cast herself from the highest mountain in all of Greece. After 10 years of suffering, 10 years of endless nightmares, it would finally come to an end. Death... ...would be her escape from madness. But it had not always been this way. Minerva’s blood was of holy descent. The sole heir to a throne of thorns, and when the fires of war engulfed her... ...she took up the sword. SLAY THEM, SLAY THE ALL! SEND THEM ALL BACK TO THE ABYSS. NO MERCY! NO QUARREL! BURN THEIR CHURCHES, BURN THEIR HOMES, LEAVE NOTHING OF THEM ABOUT. NO MEN, NO WOMEN, NO CHILDREN! KILL. CARVE THEM AN EAGLE. RIP OUT THEIR RIBS. BREAK THROUGH THEIR SPINE. DRAW THEIR LUNGS OUT. ALL FOR THE QUEEN. BEHOLD, THE QUEEN RIDES! All those who laid their lives for her she loved. And when the night comes and the killing is done, our Queen gives us plenty of fun! My tiny tin soldier boys loving you and loving being you. For when my army marches, it is I myself who walks. Can´t you see that death grows love? Can´t you see how love grows life? Be proud all my lovers, I carry your seed within me, your sons will be battle born. Soon she had replaced her army of lovers, with an army of sons. Only then was she sickened with the sight of blood. How ironic indeed, for that´s all her hands were good for. I´ve painted my soul red, and now it won´t come out no more. There is just no door where I can go through anymore. Without finding my hidden corpse, of those I have more and more. The Gods have abandoned me, now there is no hope
